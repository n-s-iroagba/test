UBIQUITOUS LANGUAGE DOCUMENT
Project: Amafor Gladiators Digital Ecosystem
Document Version: 1.0
Date: April 11, 2024
Author: Nnamdi Solomon Iroagba
Purpose: To define a shared vocabulary for all stakeholders (business, development, design) to ensure consistent understanding and communication.
CORE DOMAIN TERMS
User & Role Definitions
Term
	Definition
	Fan
	Visitor consuming public content. No account required.
	Scout
	Verified professional with 'Pro View' portal access for talent evaluation.
	Advertiser
	Verified business that creates and pays for ad campaigns.
	Patron
	Fan with recurring/lifetime financial commitment via the patronage program.
	Donor
	Fan making a one-time donation, optionally listed on the Supporter Wall.
	Content Manager
	Club staff publishing articles/videos via the no-code CMS.
	Sports Administrator
	Club staff updating fixtures/results/lineups within 30-minute SLA.
	Data Steward
	Club staff (Head of Football Ops) with exclusive edit rights to player/league data.
	Commercial Manager
	Club staff verifying advertisers, setting ad rates, handling disputes.
	IT/Security Lead
	Technical role with sole access to audit logs and security controls.
	Content & Media
Term
	Definition
	Player Profile
	Verified record with: Name, Image, DOB, Height, Position, Bio.
	Public Highlight
	Short video clip (YouTube/Vimeo) visible to all fans.
	Full Match Archive
	Complete match materials (full video, clips, gallery) available in Pro View within 30 minutes of match end.
	Article
	Published content with Title, Body, Author, Date, Tags, Featured Image.
	Tag
	Predefined category for content classification and ad targeting (Football News, Match Report, Academy Update, Player Spotlight, Club Announcement).
	Advertising & Monetization
Term
	Definition
	Ad Campaign
	Advertiser's purchased package: Creative, Target Views, Schedule, Zone.
	Ad Zone
	Fixed placement location with exact specs (Homepage Banner, Top Page Banner, Sidebar, Article Footer, Mid-Article Banner).
	Unique View
	50% of ad visible for ≥1 second, counted once per user per 24h via persistent cookie.
	Per-View Rate
	Cost per Unique View, set by Commercial Manager, with 30-day advance notice for changes.
	Ad Creative
	Image/Video file meeting zone specs, must include "Ad by [Advertiser Name], [Company Name]".
	Dispute
	Formal complaint sent to complaints@amaforgaladiatorsfc.com; initial response within 2 business days.
	Financial Support
Term
	Definition
	Donation
	One-time payment via Paystack button, with automated receipt.
	Patronage Tier
	Commitment level: Sponsor/Grand Patron (one at a time), Patron, Supporter, Advocate, Legend.
	Supporter Wall
	Public page listing donor names (opt-in), sorted alphabetically by last name (non-English names at bottom by creation date).
	Patron Recognition
	Display of patron name & portrait/logo on homepage and dedicated patron page (grouped by tier).
	System & Security
Term
	Definition
	Audit Log
	Immutable record of data changes: Old Value, New Value, User ID, Timestamp. IT/Security Lead access only.
	Cookie Consent
	GDPR banner requiring explicit 'Accept' click before tracking; 'Reject' disables tracking but allows full site use.
	Verification
	Manual approval of Scout or Advertiser accounts by club staff.
	SLA
	Maximum allowed time: Scout approval (48h), Match result update (30m), Pro View archive availability (30m), Ad dispute response (2 business days).
	Acronyms
* BRD: Business Requirements Document
* PRD: Product Requirements Document
* CMS: Content Management System
* GA4: Google Analytics 4
* SLA: Service Level Agreement
* CPV: Cost Per View
* MVP: Minimum Viable Product
* ISO: International Organization for Standardization
